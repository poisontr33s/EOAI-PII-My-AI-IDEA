# Media and ambience

The FrostGuard pitch video uses a cold, coastal, slow-burn atmosphere to match the project’s subject: invisible road danger, winter silence, and municipal systems acting before disaster becomes visible.

The ambience was aesthetically inspired by post-rock tension and long-form cinematic build-up, especially the emotional grammar associated with Godspeed You! Black Emperor-style compositions such as “The Dead Flag Blues”.

The final audio/visual material should be treated as original project media unless otherwise licensed. No third-party copyrighted music should be embedded, sampled, or redistributed without permission.
